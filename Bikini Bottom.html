About Bikini Bottom

Bikini bottom is the vibrant underwater city where spongebob squarepants and his friends live.From the krusty krab to jelyfish Fields,there's always something for happening here!

Top Attraction

- Krusty Krab:Home of the famous Krabby Patty.Don't forget to by one!
- Jelly Fish FieldsPerfect for jellyfish catching and picnics
- Goo Lagoon:A beautiful lagoon for swimming and sunbathing
- Sandy's Treedome:Visit Sandy Cheeks's treedome for a taste of Texas underwater

Travel Tips

-Bring your jellyfishing net for a fun day at JellyFish Fields
-Don't forget to try a Krabby Patty at the Krusty Krab
-Be careful around Plankton's Chum Bucket-it's not as tasty as it sounds!
